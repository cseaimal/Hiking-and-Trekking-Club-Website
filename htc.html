Four Decades of Summiting

The Hiking & Trekking Club UET Peshawar is one of the oldest adventure societies in the region. Since the early 1980s, we have served as the bridge between technical education and the raw power of nature.

"We don't just climb mountains; we build leaders who respect the wilderness as much as they challenge it."

From the lush valleys of the Galiyat to the 7,000m peaks of the Karakoram, our expeditions focus on survival, resilience, and environmental stewardship. We instill a culture of 'Leave No Trace' to ensure our mountains remain pristine for the next 45 years.
